Why Become a Mentor?

Mentors gain valuable life skills and experience they can list on their college applications. One of the key and proven success factors is the role of the mentor. More and more people want to have a lasting, profound impact in their community.

They understand that the commitment of becoming a mentor can change a child’s life while helping them grow personally. Can you remember adults who paid attention to you; who believed in you; and who guided you during your childhood? These individuals served as mentors. Wouldn’t you like to have someone think of you as their life-changing, trusted mentor?
